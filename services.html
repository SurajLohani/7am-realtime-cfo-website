Two Layers, One System

The Strategy layer finds the money. The Last Layer™ finds the leak.

Most finance audits start and stop at the top — P&L, trial balance, system records. Strategy work (Financial Diagnostic & Control Architecture) fixes what the numbers reveal. The Last Layer Approach™ goes below that, to the warehouse floor, the security guard, the delivery rider, the office boy who handled the physical document. Fraud does not live in boardrooms. It lives in the gap between what the system records and what the last layer knows.
