What You Actually Get

Every gap comes with four things — not just a label.

Here's one real example from our Playbook, shown using our demo company so you can see the exact format your report will follow.

Sample Gap · Suraj Kumar Lohani Foods Pvt. Ltd. (DEMO)

Gap #1 — Inventory Ageing Not Tracked by SKU

Problem

Fast-moving, slow-moving and dead stock are all grouped into one inventory number — there is no SKU-level ageing visibility.

Pain

Capital sits locked in slow-moving stock for months, while the business keeps borrowing working capital it shouldn't need.

Solution

A SKU-level ageing dashboard — built in Excel with Power Query, or as a live Power BI dashboard for larger catalogs — with automated fast / slow / dead classification, reviewed every month.

Expected Result

15–20% reduction in dead stock value within two quarters, freeing up working capital for faster-moving lines.

This is one sample gap, shown for format only. Your report will carry 5 gaps — specific to your business, your industry, your numbers.
